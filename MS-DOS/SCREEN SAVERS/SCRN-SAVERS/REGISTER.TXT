
                           VGAMoire 1.4b Registration
                           --------------------------

                VGAMoire is not Public Domain, nor is it FreeWare.  It
        is a ShareWare product.  This means that you can evaluate it for
        a reasonable amount of time: ten days.  After this period, you
        must decide whether you want to continue using VGAMoire.  If you
        do not want to use it anymore, then you should remove it from
        your system (or pass it on to a friend or a bulletin board
        system).  However, if you decide that VGAMoire is useful to you
        and you intend to continue using it, you must register your
        copy.  To register VGAMoire, read the instructions below (site
        licenses are available at a special price, see below).  With
        your registration, you will receive a number of special
        benefits.

                Whether you are a registered user, or just evaluating
        VGAMoire, you are strongly encouraged to give suggestions, make
        complaints, and bestow compliments.  Please be sure to report
        any problems or possible bugs to me, so that I can fix them.


                       Registration for Individual Users
                       ---------------------------------

                To register VGAMoire for use on a single machine, print
        this document and fill out the form at the end.  You have a
        choice of registering the copy you have, or of ordering the
        latest version of VGAMoire.  To simply register the version you
        have, send the completed form and a check or money order for $15
        to the address given below.  If in fact you do not have the
        latest version, you will be notified and will be given the
        option to order an upgrade for $5.

                If you would like to order the latest version right
        away, send $20 (instead of $15) and the completed form to the
        address given below.  If it happens that you already have the
        latest version, you will be notified and will be sent the next
        release when it becomes available.  Otherwise, you will be sent
        a copy of the latest version.

                See the section "Special Benefits from Registration" for
        information on why you should register and what your
        registration will get you.


                                 Site Licenses
                                 -------------

                A "site license" allows you to use VGAMoire on as many
        machines as you want or need to--provided that VGAMoire is used
        only within your "site" (an office department, a university, a
        corporation, etc).  It is much more cost-effective for both
        parties involved if an institution buys a site license.  For
        example, if an office department with 50+ microcomputers decides
        it would like to use VGAMoire on all its machines, it would be
        much more convenient and less expensive to buy a site license
        for their department.  This way, VGAMoire can be used not only
        on the existing machines, but also on any machines that are
        later added to the department, all for one price.

                To obtain a site license, print this document and fill
        out the form on the last page.  Be sure to fill out the section
        entitled "Site Licensing".  Send the completed form and a check
        or money order for the appropriate amount ($140 or $260, as
        determined by filling out the Site Licensing section of the
        registration form).

                Once a site is registered, it may obtain new versions of
        VGAMoire for $50.  The site license is extended to apply to the
        new version, once it is purchased for $50.

                See the next section, "Special Benefits from
        Registration," for information on why you should register and
        what your registration will get you.


                       Special Benefits from Registration
                       ----------------------------------

                Before you send the registration form, please take a
        moment and use the reverse side of it to list any suggestions,
        complaints, compliments, observations, or other comments you
        have regarding VGAMoire.
        
                Once your registration form is received with the
        registration fee, you will be sent a printed newsletter
        providing important information on obtaining technical support.
        The newsletter offers a discussion of the program's
        capabilities, as well as a more detailed discussion on a number
        of technical aspects and "programming notes".  In addition, the
        newsletter gives examples of how to optimally configure VGAMoire
        for your system.  Any additional late-breaking news is also
        included.  You will also receive a list of other ShareWare
        programs by Christopher Antos which you can order.

                After you have registered VGAMoire, you will be notified
        of all future releases, and will be eligible to receive them for
        a $10 fee, which may include a special discount.  The discount
        will vary, depending on the magnitude of the upgrade (if
        relatively little has been changed or improved, the discount
        will be large; if major improvements or features have been
        added, the discount will be small or non-existant).

                You will also be eligible for direct technical support,
        either by telephone, by U.S. mail, or by electronic mail.
