CONVERT ITA weights and measures utility for the Apple IIPublic Domain by Garry M. DeLong     CONVERT IT is a weights and measures converter thatoffers 50 different conversions.  Those conversions that arethe inverse of each other are grouped together forconvenience.  For example, the menu item for conversion ofINCHES TO CENTIMETERS is followed directly by that forCENTIMETERS TO INCHES.     After you enter the value that you wish to convert,press RETURN for your result.  If you want to repeat thesame conversion at this point, press ESC and you will goback one screen, allowing you to enter another value.  Infact, pressing ESC anywhere in the program will allow you toback out of any screen to the previous screen.  The onlyexception is that if you are halfway through entering anumber, you will have to backspace to the beginning of thenumber before pressing ESC.NOTE:-----     This program was written a number of years ago and wasnever published.     One of the limitations of Convert It is that some ofthe smaller results of conversions may beseverely rounded.  For example, as the result of aconversion, it is possible to get a screen that looks like:                    1.0 LI. =         .3 GALCorrect, as far as it goes.  Press <esc> and this time enter100 for a liter conversion number.  This time you will get:                    100.0 LI. =       26.4 GALYou can infer from that, then, that 1.0 Liter = 2.64 gal.NOTICES-------This program incorporates portions of The Toolbox Series, acopyrighted product of Roger Wagner Publishing, Inc.This program is intended to be purely educational and is notto be used for business, commerce, or for any othernon-educational use.The author has devoted a great deal of care to insure thatthe conversions contained herein exhibit reasonableprecision.
